## Greatest Common Divisor(GCD)

**GCD essentially is an intersection between prime factorizations of two numbers.** For example, 198 (**2** \* **3** \* **3** \* 11) and 84 (**2** \* **2** \* **3** * **7**) GCD is 6(**2** \* **3**).
